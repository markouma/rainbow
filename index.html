About Us

Rainbow Ruiru Resort is strategically located along the Nairobi- Thika Superhighway in Ruiru, 500 metres from the Eastern By-pass, 30 minutes drive from the Jommo Kenyatta International Airport and 20 minutes drive from Nairobi City Centre, with easy access to transport options and shopping destinations.

Choose from any of our 123 stylish rooms and suites, and enjoy amenities like free high-speed wireless Internet. Enjoy the delectable and creative flare within the restaurant, and then stop by our bar for perfectly poured cocktails, wine and beer.
